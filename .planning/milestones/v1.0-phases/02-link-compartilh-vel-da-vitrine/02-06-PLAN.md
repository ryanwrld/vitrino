<objective>
Replace the QR/copy placeholder with the real "Link e QR Code" panel (LOJA-03, LOJA-04, D-09–D-13). It renders a QR preview on page load for the current vitrine URL (D-11), a "Baixar PNG" button that downloads the QR as PNG (D-09, simple QR, no logo — D-10), a readonly field showing the full public URL (D-13), and a "Copiar" button that writes that exact URL to the clipboard and shows the "Link copiado!" toast (LOJA-04, D-12). Output: two helper modules, the real QR/copy panel, and two unit tests.
